Platform

What is CASTRS?

AI-Verified Catches

Upload fish photos and the AI identifies species and awards points automatically.

Compete & Rank

Collect Rare Gear

Unlock legendary rods and lures, equip them for point bonuses, build your loadout.

Getting Started

Demo Walkthrough

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with any email. Pick a username — this is your angler identity.

2

Upload Your First Catch

Tap the camera icon, snap or upload a fish photo, and watch the AI verify it and award points.

3

Visit the Shop

You start with 1,000 points. Buy a gear case — Bronze, Silver, or Gold — and open it for random loot.

4

Manage Your Inventory

Head to Gear to equip rods and lures for catch bonuses. Trade 5 items of the same rarity for a higher tier.

5

Climb the Leaderboard

Check your rank across Ontario, your local area, or among friends.

6

Customize Your Profile

Upload an avatar, equip achievement titles, and view your trophy cabinet.

7

Explore the Feed

Scroll through catches from other anglers. Like posts, drop comments, and interact.

Highlights

Features to Try

Visual Gear System

Gear cards display custom artwork by rarity — from common grays to legendary golds with glow effects.

Achievement Titles

Catch specific species to unlock titles like "Musky Magician" or "Walleye Wizard." Equip them on your profile.

Trade-In System

Combine 5 items of the same rarity into 1 item of the next tier. Clear duplicates, chase legendaries.

Friends & Clubs

Search users, send friend requests, join fishing clubs, and compete in club vs. club battles.

Notes

Demo Tips

Best on mobile. CASTRS is designed mobile-first — try it on your phone for the intended experience.

Any fish photo works. In production, AI performs real species recognition. For the demo, any fish image is accepted.

Refresh if needed. Leaderboards and feeds update in real time, but a page refresh can help if data appears stale.

Feedback welcome. Found a bug or have a suggestion? This is a work in progress — all input is appreciated.
